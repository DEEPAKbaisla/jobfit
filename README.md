JobFit — AI-Powered Interview Preparation

Stop guessing, start winning your interviews.

JobFit analyzes your resume against a job description and generates a personalized interview strategy in under a minute — including a match score, technical questions, behavioral questions, skill gap analysis, and a 7-day preparation roadmap.

---------------------------------------------------------------
Features 

Match Score — Precise compatibility score between your profile and the job requirements
Technical Questions — AI-curated questions tailored to the exact stack and role
Behavioral Questions — STAR-method questions with coaching on what interviewers look for
Skill Gap Analysis — See exactly where you fall short with severity levels (low / medium / high)
7-Day Preparation Roadmap — Daily plan with tasks, focus areas, and practice sessions
Resume PDF Generator — Generate a tailored, ATS-friendly resume PDF from your profile
